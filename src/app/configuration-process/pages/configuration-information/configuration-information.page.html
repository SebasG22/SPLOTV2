General Instructions

What are decisions ?

There are selections that MULTISPLOT will seek to meet in first grade.

What are preferences ?

Unlike decisions, MULTISPLOT gives you the opportunity to select a preference to seek to fulfill the decisions below.

How works ?

Through the use of specialized software (Linear Optimization) it will be sought to find the solution to the configuration proposed by all users.

Notes

  • The system will use the combination of decisions and preferences to offer a solution to the project.
  • A decision has a higher degree of hierarchy than a preference.
